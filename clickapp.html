the app

Earn Rewards

exclusive treats

The Caffè Frankie app is the fastest way to get your morning coffee and means you’ll never lose your loyalty card again. Link your debit or credit card for secure and speedy payment, enjoy watching your stamps build on your digital loyalty card, plus earn exclusive rewards along the way.

Avoid The Queue

click and collect

The ultimate time saver, order ahead, beat the queues and earn rewards along the way. Make sure you have the latest version of the App (this bit’s important) so you can see Click & Collect on the home screen. If not, you’ll need to update it in the App Store.

Select the store and then the items you wish to purchase. If you haven’t already, you’ll need to link a payment card to complete your order. It’ll make all future payments seamless.

We’ll send you updates and notify you when your order’s ready to collect. There’s a designated, easy-to-spot area where your coffee (or other items) will be waiting for you.
